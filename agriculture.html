Safety Warning

Agricultural Accidents: Death and injuries on farms are a serious problem. According to the HSE, on average 52 people die in agricultural accidents each year, whilst over 2,000 people suffer non-fatal injuries.

Electrical Safety: Poorly maintained electrical installations can be fatal - that's why regular checks of all electrical equipment is vital.
