Professional Electrical Services for Letting Agents

Our team are fully qualified to undertake a full range of domestic installations, all covered by an NICEIC Insurance backed warranty and on completion we will supply the relevant NICEIC test certificate.

Criminal Offence Warning

Part P Building Regulations: It is now a criminal offence if letting agents and landlords do not comply with Part P building regulations. This means that any unqualified electrician must submit plans to a building control body before commencing work.

This includes repairs, replacement, extra power or lighting points and any alterations to specialist locations such as bathrooms, kitchens and outdoors.
